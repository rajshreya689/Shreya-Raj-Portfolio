Biography

Greetings! I'm Shreya Raj, a dedicated Software Tester hailing from the vibrant city of Patna, Bihar. My academic journey began at Acrade Business College, where I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Application, followed by a Master's degree from Patna Womens College, specializing in Computer Application. These educational milestones equipped me with a solid foundation in computer science and ignited my passion for creating exceptional digital experiences.

With expertise across diverse testing methodologies, I specialize in ensuring seamless, reliable, and high-quality software solutions. My toolkit includes proficiency in manual testing, test case design, defect tracking, and basic automation. I enhance these core skills with hands-on experience in tools like Selenium and JIRA, enabling me to validate functionality, performance, and usability while delivering bug-free applications that exceed user expectations..
In pursuit of delivering high-quality and scalable software, I apply thorough testing practices to ensure reliability, performance, and user satisfaction. My proficiency extends to manual and automation testing, where I excel in designing detailed test cases, executing functional and regression tests, and using tools like JIRA and Selenium to track and optimize quality across diverse applications..
My professional journey has been enriched by hands-on testing experience and a commitment to excellence. I have contributed to diverse projects, each reflecting my ability to ensure quality and reliability. Whether it’s validating web applications or testing mobile solutions, I approach every project with precision, critical thinking, and attention to detail—striving to deliver software that exceeds client goals and user expectations

Beyond technical skills, my approach is grounded in effective communication, collaboration, and a detail-oriented mindset. My experiences have strengthened my ability to adapt quickly to new testing challenges, work seamlessly with cross-functional teams, and ensure software quality at every stage of the development lifecycle.

As I continue to evolve in the dynamic field of frontend development, I remain steadfast in exploring emerging technologies and industry trends. My goal is not only to stay ahead of the curve but also to push boundaries, innovate, and contribute to transformative digital experiences.

Join me on this journey of innovation and creativity, where every project is an opportunity to showcase expertise, elevate user experiences, and make a lasting impact in the world .
